# PsySummarise

**AI-assisted synthesis of psychiatric clinical notes into structured, clinician-ready outputs.**

---

## 🧠 Overview

PsySummarise is a research-stage health tech prototype designed to support clinicians by transforming fragmented psychiatric documentation into clear, structured summaries.

It processes multiple sources of clinical text (e.g. ward round notes, MDT entries, admission documentation) and generates high-quality outputs aligned with real NHS workflows.

---

## ⚙️ What it does

PsySummarise:
- Synthesises multiple clinical notes into a coherent clinical narrative
- Extracts key domains (risk, diagnosis, medications, progression)
- Generates structured outputs in natural clinical prose

### Current outputs:
- **Mental Health Tribunal reports**
- **Psychiatric discharge summaries**

### In development:
- Interim clinical summaries (events since last review)
- Clinic letter support
- Medication history summaries

---

## 🚀 Why it matters

Psychiatric documentation is often:
- fragmented across multiple systems
- time-consuming to review
- variable in quality and structure

PsySummarise aims to:
- reduce time spent trawling through notes  
- improve clarity and consistency of documentation  
- support clinical reasoning and decision-making  

---

## 🧩 Example use cases

- Preparing tribunal reports from multiple ward entries  
- Generating discharge summaries after inpatient admission  
- Rapidly understanding patient trajectories across services  
- Summarising key events between outpatient reviews
